Visit Tel Aviv

Tel Aviv is a vibrant and cosmopolitan city located on the Mediterranean coast of Israel. It is known for its beautiful beaches, rich history, and thriving cultural scene. From ancient ruins to modern museums, Tel Aviv has something to offer for every kind of traveler.

    White City

    The White City is a UNESCO World Heritage Site and a must-visit attraction in Tel Aviv. It is a collection of over 4,000 buildings built in the 1930s in the International Style, also known as Bauhaus. The White City is a testament to the city's rich architectural heritage and is a popular spot for architecture enthusiasts and history buffs. Visitors can take a guided tour to learn more about the history and significance of the White City. The area is also home to many cafes, restaurants, and shops, making it a great place to spend a day exploring.
