Visit Tel Aviv

Tel Aviv is a vibrant city located on the Mediterranean coast of Israel, known for its beautiful beaches, rich history, and thriving cultural scene. With its unique blend of modern and ancient architecture, Tel Aviv offers a fascinating experience for visitors. From the bustling streets of the city center to the peaceful shores of the Mediterranean, Tel Aviv has something to offer for everyone.

  • #1

    White City

    The White City is a UNESCO World Heritage Site and a must-visit attraction in Tel Aviv. This unique neighborhood is home to over 4,000 buildings designed in the Bauhaus style, making it one of the largest collections of Bauhaus architecture in the world. Visitors can take a guided tour to learn more about the history and design of the buildings, and explore the charming streets and alleys of the neighborhood. The White City is a testament to Tel Aviv's rich cultural heritage and its status as a hub for modern architecture. With its stunning buildings and picturesque streets, the White City is a photographer's paradise and a must-visit destination for anyone interested in architecture, design, and history.
