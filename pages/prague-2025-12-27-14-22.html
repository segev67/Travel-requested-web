Visit Prague

Prague is the capital city of the Czech Republic, known for its rich history, stunning architecture, and vibrant cultural scene. The city is situated on the Vltava River and is home to many famous landmarks, including the Charles Bridge and St. Vitus Cathedral. Visitors can enjoy the city's many museums, galleries, and historical sites, as well as its lively nightlife and traditional Czech cuisine.
