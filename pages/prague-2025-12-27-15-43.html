Visit Prague

Prague is the capital city of the Czech Republic, known for its rich history, cultural heritage, and stunning architecture. The city is situated on the Vltava River and is home to many famous landmarks, including Charles Bridge and Prague Castle. Visitors can explore the city's historic center, which is a UNESCO World Heritage Site, and enjoy the city's vibrant atmosphere.
