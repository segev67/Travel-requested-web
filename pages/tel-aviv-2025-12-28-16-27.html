Visit Tel Aviv

Tel Aviv is a vibrant city located on the Mediterranean coast of Israel, known for its beautiful beaches, rich history, and thriving cultural scene. Visitors can explore the city's ancient ports, visit historic landmarks, and enjoy the city's modern amenities. From its stunning architecture to its delicious cuisine, Tel Aviv has something to offer for every kind of traveler.

  • #1

    White City

    The White City is a UNESCO World Heritage Site and a must-visit attraction in Tel Aviv. It is a collection of over 4,000 buildings built in the 1930s by German Jewish architects who fled Nazi Germany. The buildings are known for their unique Bauhaus style, which features clean lines, minimal ornamentation, and an emphasis on functionality. Visitors can take a guided tour of the area to learn more about the history and architecture of the White City. The area is also home to many trendy cafes, restaurants, and shops, making it a great place to spend an afternoon.
