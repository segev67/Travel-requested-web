Visit Tel Aviv

Tel Aviv is a vibrant city located on the Mediterranean coast of Israel, known for its stunning beaches, rich history, and modern architecture. The city offers a unique blend of traditional and contemporary culture, with a thriving arts and nightlife scene. From ancient ruins to trendy neighborhoods, Tel Aviv has something to offer for every kind of traveler.

Top attractions

  • #1

    White City

    The White City is a UNESCO World Heritage Site and a must-visit attraction in Tel Aviv. It is a collection of over 4,000 buildings built in the 1930s in the Bauhaus style, which was popular among German Jewish architects who immigrated to Israel. The buildings are characterized by their clean lines, minimal ornamentation, and emphasis on functionality. Visitors can take a guided tour of the area to learn more about its history and architecture. The White City is a unique and fascinating glimpse into Tel Aviv's past, and its stunning architecture makes it a photographer's paradise.
