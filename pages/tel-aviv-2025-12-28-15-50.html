Visit Tel Aviv

Tel Aviv is a vibrant and modern city located on the Mediterranean coast of Israel. It is known for its beautiful beaches, rich cultural scene, and historic landmarks. Visitors to Tel Aviv can enjoy a wide range of activities, from relaxing on the beach to exploring the city's many museums and historical sites.

    White City

    The White City is a UNESCO World Heritage Site and a must-visit attraction in Tel Aviv. It is a collection of over 4,000 buildings built in the 1930s in the Bauhaus style, which was popular at the time. The buildings are characterized by their clean lines, minimal ornamentation, and functionality. Visitors can take a guided tour of the White City to learn more about its history and architecture. The area is also home to many cafes, restaurants, and shops, making it a great place to spend an afternoon.
