Visit Tel Aviv

Tel Aviv is a vibrant city located on the Mediterranean coast of Israel, known for its stunning beaches, rich history, and thriving cultural scene. Visitors can enjoy the city's many museums, art galleries, and performance venues, as well as its delicious cuisine and lively nightlife. From ancient ruins to modern architecture, Tel Aviv has something to offer for every interest and travel style.

    White City

    The White City is a UNESCO World Heritage Site and a must-visit attraction in Tel Aviv, featuring over 4,000 buildings designed in the International Style, also known as Bauhaus. This unique architectural style is characterized by its simplicity, functionality, and emphasis on horizontal lines and minimal ornamentation. Visitors can take a guided tour to explore the neighborhood and learn more about its history and significance. The White City is not only an important cultural landmark but also a thriving residential area, with many cafes, shops, and restaurants to discover. As one of the most iconic and influential architectural movements of the 20th century, the White City is a fascinating destination for architecture enthusiasts and anyone interested in learning more about Tel Aviv's rich cultural heritage.
