Visit Tel Aviv

Tel Aviv is a vibrant city located on the Mediterranean coast of Israel, known for its beautiful beaches, rich history, and vibrant cultural scene. The city offers a unique blend of traditional and modern attractions, from ancient ruins to modern museums and art galleries. Visitors can enjoy the city's famous markets, restaurants, and nightlife, as well as its stunning natural beauty.

    White City

    The White City is a UNESCO World Heritage Site and a must-visit attraction in Tel Aviv. It is a collection of over 4,000 buildings built in the 1930s in the International Style, also known as Bauhaus. The buildings are characterized by their clean lines, minimal ornamentation, and functionality. Visitors can take a guided tour of the area to learn more about its history and architecture. The White City is a unique and fascinating attraction that showcases Tel Aviv's rich cultural heritage.
