Visit Prague

Prague is the capital and largest city of the Czech Republic, known for its rich history, cultural landmarks, and vibrant atmosphere. The city is home to numerous museums, galleries, and historical sites, including the famous Charles Bridge and Prague Castle. Visitors can enjoy the city's unique blend of Gothic, Renaissance, and Baroque architecture, as well as its lively beer culture and traditional Czech cuisine.
