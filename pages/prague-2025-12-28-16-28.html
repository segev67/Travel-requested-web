Visit Prague

Prague is the capital city of the Czech Republic, known for its rich history, cultural landmarks, and vibrant atmosphere. The city is filled with stunning architecture, from Gothic to Art Nouveau, and offers a wide range of activities for visitors. From exploring the historic Old Town to visiting the famous Charles Bridge, Prague has something for everyone.

Top attractions

  • #1

    Charles Bridge

    Charles Bridge is a historic pedestrian bridge that spans the Vltava River, offering breathtaking views of the city. The bridge is adorned with statues and offers a unique glimpse into the city's rich history. Visitors can walk across the bridge, taking in the sights and sounds of the city, and enjoy the street performers and vendors that line the bridge. The bridge is particularly beautiful at night, when it is illuminated and takes on a magical quality. As one of the most famous landmarks in Prague, Charles Bridge is a must-visit attraction for anyone traveling to the city.
