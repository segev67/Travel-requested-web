Visit Tokyo

With its world-class restaurants, unique entertainment districts, and rich history, Tokyo is a must-visit destination for any traveler.

    The Meiji Shrine is a serene Shinto shrine located in a peaceful forested area, providing a tranquil escape from the bustling city. Dedicated to the deified spirits of Emperor Meiji and his wife, Empress Shoken, the shrine is a beautiful example of traditional Japanese architecture. The shrine's torii gate, made of Japanese cypress, is one of the largest in Japan, and the surrounding forest is home to over 100,000 trees. Visitors can take a stroll through the peaceful gardens and enjoy the serene atmosphere. The shrine is especially beautiful during cherry blossom season, when the surrounding trees are in full bloom.
