Visit Tel Aviv

Tel Aviv is a vibrant and cosmopolitan city located on the Mediterranean coast of Israel. Known for its stunning beaches, rich history, and thriving cultural scene, Tel Aviv is a must-visit destination for any traveler. From its ancient ports to its modern skyscrapers, Tel Aviv offers a unique blend of old and new that is sure to captivate and inspire.

  • #1

    White City

    The White City is a UNESCO World Heritage Site and a must-visit attraction in Tel Aviv. This unique neighborhood is home to over 4,000 buildings designed in the Bauhaus style, making it one of the largest collections of Bauhaus architecture in the world. The White City is not only a testament to Tel Aviv's rich history, but also a thriving cultural and artistic hub, with numerous galleries, museums, and performance venues. Visitors can take a guided tour of the neighborhood to learn more about its history and significance, or simply wander the streets and take in the stunning architecture. With its unique blend of history, culture, and entertainment, the White City is a truly unforgettable experience.
